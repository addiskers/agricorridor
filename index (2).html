📋 Generated Adaptation Protocol
🧬 Rhizobium Biofertilizer → Oromia Highlands
Generated for: Maize+Chickpea system · Neutral soil · Sub-humid · Amharic output
✦Strain selection: Use TAL-620 or local EIAR-sourced Mesorhizobium ciceri strains. Do not use Bradyrhizobium strains developed for soybean.
✦Application rate: 175g inoculant per 20kg seed (adjusted -12.5% from India baseline due to higher baseline soil N in Oromia clay loams).
✦Timing: Apply at planting during Belg season (Feb–April). Avoid Meher if late rains reduce rhizobial establishment.
✦Storage: Keep below 25°C in shade. Avoid highland midday sun exposure. Shelf life: 6 months from manufacture.
✦Companion input: Combine with 20kg/ha DAP (not Urea) for optimal nodulation. Urea suppresses N-fixation by 40%.
✦Water: Ensure field moisture at FC 60–80% at inoculation. Drought stress during first 3 weeks kills rhizobial colonies.

Track B: Seeds & Planting Material
Requires EIAR variety registration + phytosanitary inspection. Lead agency: DAIRE Seed Directorate
14–45 days
Step 1 · EIAR
Variety Registration
DUS test (Distinctness, Uniformity, Stability). 2-season field trial at Kulumsa or Holetta required for new varieties. CGIAR varieties fast-tracked.
Fee: ETB 12,000 · Time: 2 seasons or waived for CGIAR
Step 2 · DAIRE
Phytosanitary Inspection
Physical inspection at port of entry. Indian phytosanitary cert must be issued by NPPO (National Plant Protection Organisation India).
Fee: ETB 3,600 · Time: 3–5 days
Step 3 · EPA
Environmental Risk Assessment
Required for non-native varieties. CGIAR and ICRISAT varieties have pre-existing ERA on file — waived. New commercial varieties require ERA screening.
Fee: ETB 5,400 · Time: 7–14 days
Step 4 · ERCA
Import Permit + Duty
HS 1209 (seeds). Import duty typically 5%. VAT-exempt if destined for NGO / research use. Commercial seed import: full VAT applies.
Duty: 5% · Time: 2–3 days
⚙️
Track C: Agricultural Equipment & Machinery
Simplest track — no EPA/DAIRE needed. Lead agency: ERCA Customs + MoTRI type approval
1–5 days
Step 1 · MoTRI
Type Approval (first-time only)
Required for new machinery type entering Ethiopia for first time. Subsequent shipments of approved type: auto-cleared. Submit technical spec sheet.
Fee: ETB 8,000 · Time: 3–5 days (first time only)
Step 2 · NBE
FX Letter of Credit
For equipment >$5,000 CIF, LC required from licensed Ethiopian bank. AIBE or Commercial Bank. Platform submits pre-filled form.
Fee: 0.5% commission · Time: 1–2 days
Step 3 · ERCA
Customs Classification + Duty
Agricultural machinery (HS 8432–8436): 5% import duty + 15% VAT. Solar pump systems may qualify for 0% duty under renewable energy exemption.
Duty: 0–5% · Time: 1–2 days
Step 4 · EIC (JV only)
Investment Registration
For capital equipment brought in as JV equity contribution. Exempted from import duty. Requires EIC investment certificate. One-time.
Fee: ETB 25,000 (one-time) · Duty: 0% exempt

For Indian companies forming a local entity. Lead agencies: EIC + MoTRI + NBE + Ethiopian Revenues Authority
30–90 days

Investment Permit
Min capital $200,000 USD for foreign JV in agri-input sector. Online application via EIC portal. 51%+ Ethiopian ownership required.
Fee: ETB 3,000 · Time: 5–10 days
Step 2 · MoTRI
Business Registration
Register new JV entity as PLC (Private Limited Company). Memorandum of association, shareholder agreement, and notarised registration required.
Fee: ETB 5,000–15,000 · Time: 7–14 days
Step 3 · ERCA
TIN Registration + VAT
Tax identification number issued. VAT registration mandatory if turnover >ETB 1M/year. Takes 2–3 days at ERCA office.
Fee: Free · Time: 2–3 days
Step 4 · NBE
Bank Account + FX Approval
JV must open bank account at NBE-licensed Ethiopian bank. Indian partner capital inflow registered as FDI. Profit repatriation approved by NBE.
Fee: Bank fee · Time: 3–5 days

Agri-Input Trading License
JV must obtain trading license for each input category it handles. EPA for biologicals, DAIRE for seeds and chemicals. Annual renewal.
Fee: ETB 4,800/category/year · Time: 7–14 days

Work Permit (Indian staff)
Indian technical staff require work permit from Ministry of Labour. Max 20% foreign staff. 1-year permit, renewable. Skill transfer plan required.
Fee: ETB 12,000/person · Time: 14–30 days
